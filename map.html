Navigate with Ease

Step-by-Step Directions

Follow these detailed directions from Pruett's Grocery Store, a reliable landmark where GPS coverage is strong.

1
Start Here

Start at Pruett's Grocery Store

Use your GPS to navigate to Hochatown/Broken Bow, OK. Locate Pruett's Grocery Store at the intersection of Highway 259N and Highway 3/MLK Drive. This is your reliable starting point.

This is a great spot to stock up on groceries and essentials before heading to the cabin!
2
11.6 miles

Drive North on Highway 259

From Pruett's, drive approximately 11.6 miles North on Highway 259 until you reach Lukfata Trail Road.

Look for signs to Hochatown Distillery or Mountain Fork Brewery - same turn!
3
Turn Left

Turn Left onto Lukfata Trail Road

Turn left onto Lukfata Trail Road. This is a paved road and the same turn for Hochatown Distillery and Mountain Fork Brewery.

4
0.8 miles

Continue to the Intersection

Drive 0.8 miles down Lukfata Trail Road until you reach an intersection.

You'll cross two single-lane creek bridges. Drive carefully and watch for oncoming traffic!
5
Stay Left

Stay Far Left at the Intersection

At the intersection, stay far left to continue on North Lukfata Trail Road.

6
0.4 miles

Continue on North Lukfata Trail Road

Drive 0.4 miles on North Lukfata Trail Road. The pavement will transition to a compacted gravel road as you approach the cabin.

You're Here!

Welcome to All FORE The View!

Turn right at the cabin entrance - 1217 N. Lukfata Trail Road. The cabin sits up the driveway on the hill above the road. You've arrived at your mountain retreat!

Download Offline Maps

GPS signal can be spotty. Download the area for offline navigation before you leave.

Creek Bridges

Two single-lane bridges on route. Yield to oncoming traffic and cross carefully.

Gravel Road

The final stretch is compacted gravel. All vehicles can navigate it comfortably.
