Important Information

House Rules & Policies

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in: 4:00 PM CST or later
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM CST
  • Self check-in via keyless entry
  • Code = last 4 digits of your phone
  • Late checkout incurs additional charges

Departure Checklist

  • Load and start dishwasher
  • Start laundry with used towels
  • Turn off and clean grill if used
  • Bag trash and place in outdoor cans
  • Set thermostats (60°F winter / 76°F summer)
  • Lock all doors and windows

House Rules

  • No smoking inside cabin or patio
  • Smoking permitted in gravel areas only
  • No non-toilet-paper items in toilets
  • No pets (service animals with notice)
  • Do not move furniture
  • Extinguish all fires before leaving

Additional Guests

  • Extra guests beyond capacity: $20/person/night
  • Up to 4 additional guests allowed
  • Children under 3: Free

Pet Policy

  • No pets allowed
  • Licensed service animals permitted
  • $25/night fee for service animals
  • Advance notice required
  • Extra cleaning fee if needed

Cancellation Policy

  • 30+ days: Full refund minus $100 fee
  • 15-29 days: 50% refund
  • Under 14 days: 25% refund
  • No refunds for no-shows
  • No refunds for early departure
